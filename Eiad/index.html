Do you want to know 5 interesting facts about them?

  • Cows can smell up to 10 kilometre
  • Cows usually drink 100 litres per day
  • Pregnancy happens 9 months like humans
  • Cows only have 6-10 periods of deep sleep which are only 30 minutes. They lie around 10 hours per day
  • Compared to the hearing of humans, the hearing ability of cows is better in the deep and high frequency ranges.
  • Cows are very social animals
  • Besides the 'mooing' cows use their body language, such as the position of the head, limbs and tail, as well as facial expressions, in order to communicate.
